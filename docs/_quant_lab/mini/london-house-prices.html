What it does

Three analysis tabs for London residential property prices using 10 years of Land Registry data across 171 postcode districts.

Postcode Growth

Select a postcode district (e.g., SW11, E14) and see average price trends from 2015 to 2024 on an interactive choropleth map of London. Metrics show current price, total growth, peak year, and transaction count.

Compare Postcodes

Pick two districts and overlay their price trends on the same chart. Reveals divergent growth patterns hidden by aggregate London-wide statistics.

Brand Effect

Enter a brand name (Waitrose, Pret, Greggs) and the dashboard queries OpenStreetMap for that brand's locations in London, then compares house prices in postcode districts with vs without the brand. The classic "Waitrose effect" — quantified.
